About us

Riverbank is a club located in Utawala githunguri which is along the utawala-Githunguri access road from the Eastern bypass,we pride ourselves on fostering a warm and inclusive environment where members can come together to socialize,engage in recreational activities,and build lasting friendships.Our club is dedicated to promoting a sense of belonging and well being within the community,offering a range of events and initiatives designed to bring people together.Whether you are looking to unwind after a long day,connect with fellow residents,or simply enjoy the company of like-minded individuals RIVERBANK CLUB is the perfect place to make meaningful connections and create lasting memmories.Join us in shaping a vibrant and welcoming community right here in Utawala Githunguri

Mission and Vision

At Riverbank Club, our mission is to provide a welcoming and inclusive environment for individuals of all ages and backgrounds to engage in recreational and social activities. We envision a community where everyone can find a place to connect, learn, and thrive.



Riverbank history

Established recently, Riverbank Club has been a cornerstone of the local community for over two decades. From our humble beginnings as a small gathering of outdoor enthusiasts, we have grown into a vibrant hub for recreation



Team and leadership

Our dedicated team comprises passionate individuals who are committed to creating enriching experiences for our members. Led by our founder, Moses mbweke, our team brings together a diverse range of talents and expertise to ensure that every aspect of the club reflects our values and commitment to excellence.



Values and Culture

At Riverbank Club, we uphold values of inclusivity, respect, and community engagement. Our culture is defined by a spirit of camaraderie, mutual support, and a deep appreciation for the natural environment that surrounds us.



Achievements and Milestones

Over the years, Riverbank Club has garnered recognition for its contributions to the community, including awards for environmental stewardship and our commitment to promoting healthy, active lifestyle
